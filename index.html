Film photography - especially black and white - exists in a state of beauty. There is a draw that seeks to bring in anyone who glances at the work. Finding these moments, these inescapable blips in time is what draws me to share my photography and bring that beauty to all others.

About

I started to shoot film as a way to take a step back from the constant shooting on mobile devices. Stepping back, intentionally, to a time and place where every shot is intentional and the results are not instant has struck a chord with me. I am living in a way to create art - but not using the tools of abundance, but rather fostering the limited nature of a roll of film.

My work leans toward quiet observation — street, landscape, and the in-between moments most cameras are pointed away from.

Prints are available on request. If something here stops you, I'd like to hear about it.
